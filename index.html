About the Team

DOMINATING BOYS is a dedicated kabaddi team inspired by the virtues of Lord Ram and the strength of Lord Hanuman. We embody the character and integrity exemplified by Ram Ji, and channel the immense strength and determination of Hanuman Ji. Our commitment to excellence and sportsmanship drives us to train hard and dominate the field, always playing with skill, strategy, and teamwork. Embracing the spirit of strength and wisdom like Hanuman, we are DOMINATING BOYS, a relentless force in the world of Kabaddi. Our team is built on the pillars of dedication, discipline, and a deep-rooted passion for the sport. Each player is driven by an unwavering commitment to push their limits, exemplifying the courage and resilience of our inspirations. We aim to dominate the kabaddi field with our exceptional teamwork, strategic prowess, and unparalleled skill. Our journey is fueled by the blessings of Ram Ji and Hanuman Ji, guiding us to strive for greatness and honor in every match we play. जय हनुमान ज्ञान गुण सागर। जय कपीस तिहुँ लोक उजागर।।
